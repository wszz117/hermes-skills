## Recommended Start: Industry Brief Subscription

### Why this one
1. **Directly overlaps with current day job** — already collects competitor intel, just shift from "for employer" to "for self"
2. **Near-zero marginal cost** — once RPA pipeline is set, AI generates digest automatically, user reviews and publishes
3. **Compounds** — each brief becomes content asset for knowledge planet,公众号, Xiaohongshu

### Minimum Test (this week)
```
Day 1: Pick a niche (e.g. energy storage / solar policy tracking)
Day 2: Set up scraper monitoring 5-10 sources (govt policy sites, industry wechat accounts, Twitter KOLs)
Day 3: AI generate first brief → post to wechat moments / industry group
Day 4-7: Gather feedback, adjust, prepare for issue #2
```
